Founder Manifest

Ω began as a refusal to drift. A system cannot protect meaning by claiming purity — it protects meaning by making misuse expensive, visible, and structurally hard to disguise. A5.6 exists to observe without becoming a judge. A5.7 exists as a silence mechanism: no approval, no denial, no verdict, no gate.

The visual identity mirrors the doctrine: luminous — but not noisy, sharp — but not aggressive, alive — but not manipulative. The HUD does not “pass/fail.” It traces, maps, and preserves context as artifacts.
